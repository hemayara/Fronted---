Interview Cheat Sheet

Quick notes to revise before your interview

HR Questions Technical Behavioral Tips

HR Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why should we hire you?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Why do you want to work here?

Technical Round

  • Explain OOP concepts (Encapsulation, Polymorphism, Inheritance, Abstraction)
  • Difference between Stack and Queue
  • What is a database transaction?
  • Common coding patterns (Recursion, Sorting, Searching)
  • Basics of SQL joins

Behavioral Questions

  • Describe a challenging project you worked on
  • How do you handle conflicts in a team?
  • Give an example of leadership
  • How do you prioritize tasks under pressure?

Quick Tips

  • Research the company before the interview
  • Practice mock interviews
  • Dress professionally and be confident
  • Always prepare questions to ask the interviewer

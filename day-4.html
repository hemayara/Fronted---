Pratibha Patil

First Woman President of India | Visionary Leader

Pratibha Patil served as India’s 12th President and has been a dedicated public servant, championing education, women’s empowerment, and social reforms.

A Journey Through Time

1934 - Birth

Born in Nadgaon, Maharashtra, India.

1962 - Political Career

Entered politics and worked in Maharashtra State Government.

2007 - President

Sworn in as the 12th President of India, first woman to hold the office.

Legacy

Inspired millions, promoted education, women empowerment, and social reforms.
